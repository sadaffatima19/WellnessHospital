Our Departments

Explore our diverse medical departments, each committed to providing exceptional care and services. From cardiology to pediatrics, our specialized teams are here to support your health and well-being.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department offers comprehensive heart care, including advanced diagnostics, treatments, and surgeries.

Neurology

The neurology department specializes in treating disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedics department provides expert care for musculoskeletal conditions, including surgeries and rehabilitation.

Dermatology

The dermatology department offers treatments for skin conditions, including advanced laser therapies and cosmetic procedures.

Pediatrics

The pediatrics department is dedicated to the health and well-being of children, from newborns to adolescents.

General Surgery

Our general surgery department performs a wide range of surgical procedures with a focus on minimally invasive techniques.

Endocrinology

The endocrinology department provides comprehensive care for hormonal disorders and metabolic conditions.

Gastroenterology

Our gastroenterology department offers expert care for digestive system disorders, including advanced endoscopic procedures.

Rheumatology

The rheumatology department specializes in treating autoimmune diseases and disorders affecting joints and muscles.
